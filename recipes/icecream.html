Homemade Icecream

This easy homemade vanilla ice cream recipe is a delightful treat for any occasion. Creamy and rich, it's a classic favorite that you can enjoy on its own or as a base for your favorite toppings and mix-ins.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream and whole milk until well combined.
  2. Add the granulated sugar and continue to whisk until the sugar is completely dissolved. This will make the ice cream base sweet and creamy.
  3. Stir in the pure vanilla extract, ensuring it's evenly distributed throughout the mixture. This is what gives your ice cream its delightful vanilla flavor.
  4. Pour the ice cream mixture into an ice cream maker and follow the manufacturer's instructions for churning. Typically, this will take about 20-25 minutes.
  5. Once the ice cream has thickened and reached a soft-serve consistency, you can enjoy it immediately or transfer it to an airtight container and freeze for a firmer texture.
  6. If you choose to freeze the ice cream, allow it to set in the freezer for at least 2-4 hours or until it's your preferred level of firmness.
  7. Serve your homemade vanilla ice cream on its own or with your favorite toppings and syrups. Enjoy your delightful treat!
